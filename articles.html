Al-Safir hair transplantation technique

The Sapphire hair transplantation technique is a relatively modern technique that uses the Sapphire pen to open the channels in which the follicles are transplanted. This technology has many advantages compared to other traditional techniques, such as: Higher precision: The Sapphire gemstone pen allows finer-looking channels to be opened, resulting in a more natural appearance of the transplanted hair. Less stress on tissue: The Sapphire Pen causes less stress to tissue than metal blades, resulting in a shorter recovery period and less pain. LESS RISK OF BLEEDING: The Ambassador pen tends to bleed less than metal blades, reducing the risk of infection. Al-Safir hair transplantation technique is very similar to FUE hair transplantation technique, where hair follicles are extracted from the donor area one by one using fine needles. However, the Sapphire technique uses the Sapphire pen to open channels in the recipient area, rather than using metal blades. The hair transplantation process using the Sapphire technique includes the following steps: The surgeon administers local anesthesia to the scalp area. The surgeon uses fine needles to extract hair follicles from the donor area. The surgeon opens fine channels in the recipient area using the Sapphire pen. The surgeon implants hair follicles into the open channels. The hair transplantation process using the Sapphire technique usually takes between 4 to 8 hours, depending on the size of the area being transplanted. The Sapphire hair transplantation technique has many advantages over other traditional techniques, making it a good choice for people looking for natural results and a shorter recovery period..

Hair transplantation using DHI technology

DHI hair transplantation is a relatively new technique that uses a Choi Implanter Pen to implant hair follicles directly into the recipient area. This technology has many advantages compared to other traditional techniques, such as: Higher precision: The Choi pen allows hair follicles to be transplanted with greater precision, resulting in a more natural appearance of the transplanted hair. Greater speed: The surgeon can transplant more hair follicles in less time using the Choi pen, reducing the duration of the procedure. Less pain: The Choi Pen causes less pain than traditional techniques, making the procedure more comfortable for the patient.
